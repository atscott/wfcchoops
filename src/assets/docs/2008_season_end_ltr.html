Dear Wrightstown Full-Court Club players and parents:

Congratulations to all of our WFCC teams and players that played this season! All six of our teams were very successful. I want to personally thank all the coaches and parents for all of their time associated with our teams. Great work by all!

With the WFCC seasons concluding, the basketball season does not end now though. There are even more opportunities for your child to play and improve their overall basketball skills this off-season. I really hope that all our WFCC players take advantage of our District's summer school program and Tiger Camp. Please check out and utilize our WFCC website at www.wfcchoops.com for updated information regarding our Club and other basketball related opportunities.

SUMMER SCHOOL

I am teaching the 3rd/4th grade, 5th/6th grade, and 7th/8th grade boys' basketball summer school courses our District offers. I sincerely hope to see your son sign up for these courses. These courses are high energy and are a perfect blend of fundamental development and game strategies. Dates of summer school are June 9 to June 27.

15,000 SHOT CLUB

Congrats to Andrew Scott, Kurt Jansen, Brandon Do-McKenzie, Michael Falck, Jordan Johnson, Cody Lukes, Craig Nennig, Bryce Buboltz, and Nathan Larson! These players filled out the proper paperwork to prove that they completed the 15,000 Shot Club requirements from last off-season. By doing so, their names will be added to the plaque in the HS trophy case, receive a t-shirt, and an individual award. Your son will receive more information on this shot club in summer school courses for this current off-season. It is an excellent way to track your off-season shooting and earn an extra reward for an awesome effort!
